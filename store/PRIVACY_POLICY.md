# ShopSphere Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

ShopSphere ("we", "our", or "us") operates the ShopSphere mobile application. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Name, email address, and phone number (optional) when you register
- Authentication credentials managed securely by Firebase Authentication

### Usage Data
- App interactions collected via Firebase Analytics (screens viewed, purchases, searches)
- Crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics to improve stability
- Performance metrics via Firebase Performance Monitoring

### Device Information
- Push notification tokens (FCM) for order updates and promotions
- Device identifiers for App Check fraud prevention

### Order & Shopping Data
- Cart contents, wishlist items, order history, and shipping addresses
- Stored locally on your device and synced to Cloud Firestore when signed in

## How We Use Your Information

- Provide and maintain the shopping experience
- Process orders and send order status notifications
- Personalize product recommendations
- Improve app performance, security, and reliability
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage & Security

- Passwords are never stored in the app or our databases
- Sensitive tokens use secure storage mechanisms
- Firestore and Storage are protected by role-based security rules
- Firebase App Check helps prevent unauthorized API access

## Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase services:
- Authentication, Firestore, Storage, Cloud Messaging
- Analytics, Crashlytics, Performance, Remote Config, App Check

See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Your Rights

You may:
- Update your profile information in the app
- Request account deletion by contacting support@shopsphere.app
- Opt out of push notifications via device settings

## Children's Privacy

ShopSphere is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
